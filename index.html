About our company

NPO Perspektiva ltd is Ukrainian agricultural commodities export company that securely delivers pulses and oil seeds, fruits and berries worldwide. We are the producer that establishes long-term relationships with our clients & deliver commodities to our buyers, importers, whole-sellers, retailers all around the world providing highly competitive prices and top quality to our customers with variety of products.
